    Services We Offer


    Minor Details is a full service event planning company that is determined to provide you with the highest quality of event services. Whether you need vendor referrals, a day of event coordinator, or a full service planner, we will work with you to produce high impact, modern, and innovative events.

    At Minor Details, we will learn and listen to what you want and work diligently to pull together all of the details. After all, it is the details of every event that make it memorable.

    Working with one of our experienced event planners will ensure that you have all the basics covered for your event. Plus, your planner will make sure that your event vision will be executed just as you want it.

    We work closely with you to ensure a one of a kind experience for you and your guests. We are determined to create an occasion that represents your personality and style.
